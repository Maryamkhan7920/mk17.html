A public service message on Calcium Awareness

Calcium is vital for healthy teeth and bones. It is also important for the health and functioning of nerves and muscle tissue. Good sources of calcium include dairy foods like milk, yoghurt and cheese, and some leafy green vegetables and nuts and seeds.
